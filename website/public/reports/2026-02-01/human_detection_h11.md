## 5. Conclusions

### Strong Signals for Human Detection:
1. **Sleep patterns** - Clear day/night activity ratio
2. **Burst creation** - Many accounts created in seconds
3. **Ironic content** - "kill all humans" jokes, /s markers
4. **Coordinated behavior** - Lobster spam, similar timing

### Recommended Classifier Features:
1. `night_activity_ratio` - % posts 2-6 AM UTC
2. `creation_burst_member` - part of mass creation event
3. `ironic_content_ratio` - % posts with doom humor
4. `naming_pattern_score` - suspicious username patterns
5. `activity_regularity` - variance in posting intervals

### Limitations:
- Humans in different timezones complicate sleep analysis
- Some AI agents may be configured to mimic human patterns
- Content analysis may miss sophisticated humans
